who are we.

CHROMATIC is a creative studio for your every color. Founded in 2021, we are determined to give a new perspective to design.

Our founders met at college, when they started their bachelor’s degree in Multimedia & Graphic Design and have been inseparable since!

what we do.

At Chromatic, we specialize in crafting designs that are fueled by creativity, energy, and a sense of fun. Our studio is all about bringing vibrant and imaginative concepts to life, whether through branding, digital content, or print materials. We believe that graphic design should not only be visually appealing but also create an enjoyable and memorable experience.

Our process combines strategic thinking with a playful, innovative approach. We love experimenting with bold colors, dynamic typography, and unique ideas to ensure that each project reflects the individuality of our clients while also captivating their audience.
